Oracle

The Oracle Social Marketing Cloud provides brands and agencies with the tools to execute social marketing strategies.

It is a component of the Oracle Social Marketing Cloud, which through high profile and strategic acquisitions (Eloqua, Responsys, BlueKai, etc.) has become an industry leading marketing cloud solution.

Unifying Acquisitions

Oracle acquired Vitrue, Involver, and Collective Intellect during the summer of 2012 with the intent of combining the 3 platforms into a single social marketing platform that would function as a single solution for all social Listening, Engagement, Monitoring, and Messaging needs.

During the spring of 2013 I managed the execution of the first phase of the unification process which gave the platform a unified feel by creating a common UI across the many applications making up each platform.

This first phase was extremely important for bringing the three acquisitions together as a single team and platform and also laid the groundwork for more difficult but powerful integrations at lower application layers and was also received very well by the market.

Integrating Platforms

The second and third phases of the unification initiative were to centralize all user administration and SSO into a single application and then to centralize all business object management in the same application.

It was decided that all users and objects would be administered from the former Vitrue administration application and so it was my responsibility to manage the execution of the final 2 phases as well. Despite many technical, organizational, and political challenges, the platforms were fully consolidated in early 2014.

The three phase project (UI integration, User/Account integration, and Data Objects integration) required participation from teams across 4 geographical locations (Atlanta, Austin, San Francisco, and Boulder) and resulted in the Oracle Social Marketing Cloud's defining competitive feature; being a one stop shop for all major Social Marketing functionality.
